Getting Started

  1. Make sure you have a GitHub account.
  2. Go to Google Domains, and buy a domain. Basic domains are 12/yr.

    3. Setting up Your Domain

  3. On Google Domains, go to the side menu and click My Domains.
  4. Select the domain you are using for this course.
  5. Navigate to the side menu and select DNS.
  6. Change the following:
    1. Host Name: nmi
    2. Type: CNAME
    3. TTL: 60
    4. Data: githubUsername.github.io.
  7. Refer to the following tutorial.

    8. Adding your Custom Domain

  8. Enter your repository and go to settings.
  9. Click on the Pages tab on the left-hand side.
  10. Scroll until you find Custom Domain
  11. Enter your custom domain without the www. at the beginning, and hit save. The DNS check should begin, and it should soon turn green.
  12. Check the box that says Enforce HTTPS.
  13. Congratulations, your site is now live at nmi.customDomain!
